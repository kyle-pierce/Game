A bit of information on the classes:

Warrior: A warrior specializes in melee weapons, both two handed
weapons and one handed weapons.  They also can also gain skill
with shields. Their primary attribute is Strength.

Ranger: A ranger dissects their enemies with a bow and arrow.
They also can weild daggers for use in deadly sneak attacks and
brutal hand-to-hand combat.  Their primary attribute is Agility.

Mage: A mage weilds the power of the elements to reign destruction
on their foes.  They use raw magic or that channeled through a wand
or stave.  Their primary attribute is Magicka.
